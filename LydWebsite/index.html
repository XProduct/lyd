At Lyd, we feel when listening to high resolution audio, we deserve a high quality expierence while browsing and playing our music library. That's where Lyd came from. We have finally fulfilled the need for a beautifully designed and seemless audio player for everyone.

Freedom with Lyd Remote

Sitting at a computer or sitting in the most comfortable spot next to your amplifier, you should have the freedom to sit where you want and listen to your favorite tunes. With Lyd Remote you can control the music from your computer, from anywhere in your house on a mobile device.

Technical

Supported Formats: flac, wav, ogg

Lyd Remote uses your local network to control music that resides on a primary listening computer from a device with a web browser.
